Tribute Page for Mia


Mia had a rough start in life. She bounced from home to home. It did not take long for that beautiful smile and love of life to melt my heart.


Not only was Mia allowed on the furniture, she had her very own chair. She was the biggest of all lap dogs.


Mia's favorite things to do were to swim, play frisbee, and be with her people.
